        Rule Cheatsheet
        • Goal: be the last player with dice remaining.
        • On your turn, you either Bid, call Dudo, or call Calza.
        • Bids must legally increase from the previous bid.
        • Normal rounds: Pacos (1s) are wild for non-1 bids.
        • Palifico (when someone has 1 die): opening bid sets a locked value and Pacos are not wild.
        • Dudo: challenge the previous bid; loser of the challenge loses a die.
        • Calza: only with 3+ players and not in Palifico; exact count gains a die, otherwise lose one.
        • When a player reaches 0 dice, they are eliminated.
